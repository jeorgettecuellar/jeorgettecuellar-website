I train organizations to build resource navigation systems so youth can access support effectively and consistently.

What I Do

Many organizations have resources available, but staff often lack a clear system for finding, organizing, and tracking them. This leads to inconsistent referrals and gaps in service.

I provide step-by-step frameworks and tools that teams can immediately use to improve how youth access support.

Workshops & Trainings

Resource Navigation Systems

Learn how to find, vet, and organize resources across housing, education, legal support, and more.

Includes a structured database template and workflow that can be used across staff.

Referral Tracking & Follow-Through

Build systems to track referrals and ensure youth successfully access services.

Learn how to implement follow-up checkpoints and improve program outcomes.

What Organizations Gain

  • Improved consistency across staff
  • Reduced gaps in service delivery
  • Clear systems for tracking resources and referrals
  • Tools that remain usable despite staff turnover

About

As a young person navigating foster care, Jeorgette learned that access to resources often depended on whether someone knew where to look. That experience now informs her work helping organizations build systems that make support more accessible and consistent for youth.
